Cat's tongue cookie

A cat's tongue cookie is a type of cookie shaped like a cat's tongue (long and flat) It is also a popular snack in Indonesia where it is known as Lidah Kucing.

Cat's tongue cookies made from dough of wheat flour mixed with butter, powdered sugar, and egg are then baked in the oven until cooked.

A cat's tongue mold pan may be used in their preparation, in which cookie dough is placed and then baked.
